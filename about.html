About Our Church

Worship · Mission · Discipleship

Cornerstone Slavic Bible Church is a bilingual (Russian/English) Baptist church in Bellingham, WA. We are dedicated to worshipping God, preaching the Gospel, and making disciples of all nations.

Our Mission

To glorify God through worship, discipleship, fellowship, service to others, and preaching the Gospel.

Statement of Faith

We believe in the foundational truths of the Christian faith as revealed in Holy Scripture.

1 Scripture

The Bible is the God-breathed, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, the sole rule of faith and practice.

2 God

One God, eternally existing in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — Creator of all things.

3 Jesus Christ

The deity of the Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and ascension.

4 Salvation

Salvation is the gift of God's grace, received through faith in Jesus Christ — not by works.

5 The Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit convicts the world of sin, regenerates believers, and empowers them for holy living and service.

6 The Church

The Church is the Body of Christ. The local church is a congregation of baptized believers for worship, edification, and service.

7 The Second Coming

The personal, visible, and glorious return of the Lord Jesus Christ, the resurrection of the dead, and the final judgment.
