Plan Your Visit

We are glad to welcome every guest!

Service Schedule

Language

Our services are bilingual — conducted in both Russian and English.

Translation headphones are available for English-speaking guests. Just ask when you arrive — we are happy to provide them.

We strive to make everyone feel welcome, regardless of language background.

What to Expect

Our worship services include:

  • Congregational hymn singing
  • Reading and preaching of God's Word
  • Prayer
  • Fellowship over tea after the service

Atmosphere

We are a friendly, family-oriented church. Come as you are.

Dress code — casual or dressy — whatever you are comfortable with.

Children are welcome! We have a children's ministry.
