Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions

When are the worship services?

Sunday: 10:00 AM — Sunday Worship Service
Friday: 7:00 PM — Friday Evening Service
Wednesday: 7:00 PM — Youth Ministry (SybYouth)
Tuesday: 7:00 PM — Bible Study / Teens Group

What language are the services in?

Our services are bilingual — conducted in both Russian and English. We strive to make everyone feel welcome and comfortable.

What does the church believe?

We believe the Bible is the infallible Word of God, in the Trinity, and in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ. Our full statement of faith is available on the page.

Is there a children's ministry?

Yes! We have Sunday school for children of various ages. Children attend their own classes during the main worship service in separate rooms.

Where can I watch the livestream?

Our worship services are streamed on YouTube. Visit the Livestream page to watch.

How should I prepare for my first visit?

Come as you are! There is no dress code. Learn more about what to expect on the Plan Your Visit page.

How can I contact the church?

You can call us at (360) 756-8338 or email church@csbchurch.com. You can also fill out the form on the Contact page.
